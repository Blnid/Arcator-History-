Tale of Chickens; Mooshroom Island

one day in that empty forest surrounded by just my tall cobble tower housing the signs and the single house on the mountain I looked down and saw, a single egg I don't know where that egg came from but I ran back to my house in front of the signs of the ancients I dug a hole 2 deep and threw the egg inside a baby chicken emerged !! I went back to the jungle, cutting some more came back the chicken grew laid some eggs I threw the eggs back at the hole more babies emerged each day I went back to the jungles gathering a full chest then doubles of logs "maybe one day this will be useful to someone" while the chicken hole grew eventually I brought seeds and they grew faster I gathered up two stacks of eggs and walked back outside the tower looking at the silent forest I aimed my camera up into the sky held down right click- and listened as each egg flew out of my hand 32 eggs later arcing off into the distance they disappeared behind the trees I never saw where they landed but each time I now repeated that coming back to them getting their eggs and throwing them around the next day I was walking back to my home, in the tower when I passed through the forest I saw chickens walking around there was sound again the map was slowly awakening on one of my journeys out to search for relics I came across a very strange village the buildings looked..custom as I got closer I saw an enchantment table half surrounded by books I was shocked the rest of the map was stone tools what was this I eventually found one home full of double chests with iron and a few diamonds they're rich- and inside I saw signs written in a foreign language I couldn't understand still the village was cool they had a big bridge over a river and I just knew whoever these people were they made it further than the other ghosts I went back home again on the 25th of august I wanted to search for more relics so I packed up some blocks plenty of wood and set out I ran 500 blocks through the silent forest past the mountain that looked down upon the mysterious village I ran through the plains 2000 blocks and through another chain of mountains 4000 blocks I soon realized maybe, I had gone too far to find things if I wanted to find relics they would be close to spawn not this far out but I had to keep going- at 6000 blocks I reached water it was a small inlet I crossed it and walked through a short jungle ahead of me lay the ocean I made a boat it was nightfall but even monster spawning was bugged I set out I sailed another 1000 blocks far distant and alone eventually I saw a tiny island hardly 10 x 10 blocks long and wide I dug up some dirt and made a simple pixel art HI maybe one day someone would see it I got my boat and kept going I sailed past some more islands but at 7000 blocks something told me to turn so I turned 45 degrees and kept going and then by pure luck I saw a massive island in the distance I dismounted my boat it was a mooshroom island under dusk I walked around there were mooshrooms it was interesting to me the silent forest was the first few hundred blocks by 1000 blocks, I saw animals again that hadn't been killed now, 7000 blocks and feeling at the ends of the earth I walked an island no one had likely found and it was peaceful I knew monsters couldn't spawn but something felt poetic that on this island I was perfectly safe technically I could get unlimited soup I would never starve I could "live" here forever but if I stayed I would never ever find the relics I would be far from the tower of signs or the tale of chickens this had life but it wasn't worth living eventually the sun began to come back rising over the water in the same direction I had been travelling as if asking me to keep going forward instead using the buggy commands I knew I typed /spawn and the server counted You are now teleporting Move to cancel 5 4 I thought to myself "I can cancel right now, and stay a bit longer" 3 2 "no, I need the relics" "the history" 1 the map flashed loaded and then I found myself back home to the sound of chickens in the hole the search for history had to continue
